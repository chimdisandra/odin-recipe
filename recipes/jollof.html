Egg Sauce

Description

Jollof Rice is a classic West African delicacy. It has its origins in Senegal but is also a staple in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. Jollof rice is usually served with a side of coleslaw or plantains.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • 2 large red onions
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • 1kg of meat(Beef or Chicken)
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Stock Cubes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps for preparing Jollof Rice

  1. Blend tomatoes,1 onion, scotch bonnet peppers,ginger and garlic into paste and set aside.
  2. Wash the beef and place into a pot. Season with salt, stock cubes, curry, thyme and some chopped onion.
  3. Add water to the meat and boil for about 40 minutes.
  4. Heat some vegetable oil in a pot. Add chopped onions and garlic and fry until golden brown.
  5. Pour the tomato paste into the pot and fry for about 20 minutes. Stir frequently to avoid burning.
  6. Add the beef stock to the tomato mixture, add a pinch of salt, some curry and thyme and stir.
  7. Add some parboiled rice into the tomato mixture, bay leaves and cook on low heat
  8. Serve when ready.
