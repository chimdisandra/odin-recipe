Catfish Pepper Soup

Description

Catfish Pepper Soup is a popular Nigerian delicacy commonly served as staple at bars and restaurants.It is usually served piping hot and can be eaten alone or with a side of yams or a local gelatinous maize pudding known as Agidi.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sized Catfish
  • 1 medium sized Onion
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cameroon Pepper
  • Pepper Soup spice
  • Scent leaves
  • Utazi leaves
  • 2 Stock/Bouillon cubes
  • Salt
  • Yams(optional)

Steps for making Catfish Pepper Soup

  1. Wash the fish with salt and hot water to remove slime and then cut into appropriate sized pieces.
  2. Slice the onions,scent leaves,utazi leaves and yams and then set aside.
  3. Put the washed fish into a pot; add stock cubes, cameroon pepper,sliced onions, a few pieces of yam and salt to taste.
  4. Add a few cups of water, ensuring that it covers the ingredients and bring the mixture to a boil for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the scent and utazi leaves, pepper soup spice (and more pepper if not satisfied with the spice level) and boil for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Set down the pepper soup and serve hot!
