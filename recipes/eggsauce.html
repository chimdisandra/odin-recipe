Egg Sauce

Description

Egg sauce (also known as Egg stew) is an egg and tomato based dish. It is usually served with boiled/fried yams or potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Habanero peppers
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Stock/Bouillon cubes

Steps for making Egg Sauce

  1. Chop tomatoes, peppers, onions and garlic and set aside.
  2. Break eggs into a bowl, add some salt, whisk and set aside.
  3. Heat some vegetable oil in a frying pan, add the chopped garlic and onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, peppers and stock cubes and fry on medium heat for about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Pour the eggs into the tomato mixture and stir continously to avoid burning.
  6. Serve while hot.
